Pre-Design and Planning

Architrevo has assisted sophisticated clients who know what they want and how to get there. Likewise, Architrevo also assists those clients for whom this might be their first and only building or fit-up project, and services provided are matched to each client’s unique requirements. Architrevo believes the pre-design effort is critical to a project’s success. Without it, the risk is a false start. Planning as well allows us bring together a team of architectural designers and landscape designers. Through this collaborative team, we help clients through the creation of masterplans with a multitude of stakeholders to refine a unified vision for the project, and then frame that vision in a way that moves the project forward.
